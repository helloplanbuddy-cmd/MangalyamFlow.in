Common Questions

You asked, we answered

Everything you need to know before trusting Shaadi OS with the most important financial decision of your life.

How accurate are the budget estimates?▼
Our budget benchmarks are derived from real Indian wedding data across 23+ cities. They represent market ranges, not guaranteed prices — actual costs vary by vendor and season. Think of it as having a knowledgeable friend who's attended 10,000 weddings: they know the range, the traps, and the tricks. Always verify specific quotes with vendors.
Is this just ChatGPT with a wedding prompt?▼
No. Generic AI tools give you static text responses with no cost logic. Shaadi OS has a financial intelligence layer built specifically for Indian weddings — including city-specific pricing benchmarks, overspend detection by category, and dynamic budget reallocation.
Do I need to create an account?▼
Your basic plan is completely free and requires no account. To save your plan, access vendor contacts, or download the PDF report, you'll need to create a free account — which takes under 30 seconds. We never share your data with vendors or third parties.
My wedding is 6 months away — is it too late?▼
6 months is actually ideal. Many couples find their biggest savings opportunities by using Shaadi OS before signing any contracts. If you've already booked some vendors, we can still identify remaining optimization opportunities and alert you to upcoming spending traps.
What if my budget or guest count changes?▼
Your plan updates instantly when you change any input. The free plan lets you regenerate as many times as you want. The CFO plan includes a live dashboard where changes automatically recalculate your entire budget and alert you to new risks.
This is a big life event. Can I really trust a tool with it?▼
We understand the weight of this decision completely. Shaadi OS doesn't replace your judgment — it gives you better information so your decisions are well-informed. Think of it as the financial clarity you'd have if you had a trusted advisor who'd planned hundreds of weddings. The final decisions are always yours.
